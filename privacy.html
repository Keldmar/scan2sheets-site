Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Scan2Sheets app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Oleksandr (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".


What the Application Does

Scan2Sheets is a mobile application that lets users digitize printed or handwritten records by capturing them with the device camera and writing the recognized data directly into the user's own Google Sheets spreadsheets. To do this, the Application authenticates the user with their Google account and accesses their Google Sheets spreadsheets through the official Google APIs.

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
  • The time spent on the Application
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data or provide features.

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that the Service Provider request will be retained by them and used as described in this privacy policy.


Information Accessed Through Your Google Account

When you sign in with Google, the Application receives the following data from Google:

  • Your Google account email address, display name, and a unique account identifier — used solely to identify you within the app session.
  • An OAuth access token issued by Google that allows the Application to act on your behalf for the scopes you grant. Tokens are kept in memory by the Android Credential Manager and Google Play Services and are not persisted by the Application to disk.

The Application requests the following Google API scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets — used to read from and write to spreadsheets you explicitly select inside the Application. This includes creating new spreadsheets, appending rows produced by a scan, updating existing rows when you correct recognition results, and deleting rows you mark for deletion.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly — used solely to list the names and identifiers of your Google Sheets spreadsheets so you can pick which one to use inside the Application. The Application reads only spreadsheet metadata (file id and display name) and does not download, modify, share, or delete any other Google Drive content.

The Application also uses the device camera to capture documents. Captured images are processed locally on your device. Images are not uploaded to any server controlled by the Service Provider. After recognition, the extracted text is written to your selected Google Sheets spreadsheet via the official Google Sheets API.
