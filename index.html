Scan2Sheets

Digitize printed and handwritten records straight into your Google Sheets.

What it does

Scan2Sheets is a mobile application that lets you capture printed or handwritten records with your device camera and write the recognized data directly into your own Google Sheets spreadsheets — no manual typing, no copy-paste between apps.

How it works

  • Sign in with your Google account.
  • Pick an existing spreadsheet from your Google Drive, or create a new one inside the app.
  • Point the camera at a document — the app recognizes the text and prepares a row for review.
  • Confirm or correct the data — the app appends a new row to your spreadsheet.

Privacy

Scan2Sheets only accesses the Google Sheets you explicitly select. Your captured images are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server.
